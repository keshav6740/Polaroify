Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2, 2026

Overview

Polaroify lets users generate music posters using third-party APIs (Spotify and lyric providers).

Data We Process

We process search queries, selected song/album metadata, optional custom image uploads for poster rendering, and basic usage analytics events to improve product quality.

What We Do Not Collect

We do not require account signup and we do not collect passwords or payment details on this site.

Analytics and Logs

We store minimal operational logs and event data (for example, search and export events) for monitoring, reliability, and product improvement.

Third-Party Services

Polaroify uses Spotify Web API and lyric providers. Their privacy terms may apply when using related content.

Contact

For privacy questions, contact the site owner through the project repository or business contact channel.
